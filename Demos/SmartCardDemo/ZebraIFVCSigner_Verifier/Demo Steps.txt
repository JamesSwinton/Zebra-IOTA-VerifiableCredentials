Pre Requisite:

    1. Health Authroity has an Identity on the IOTA Tangle

    2. Health Authroity has provided vaccination to patient

/**********************************************************/

Creating & Verifying Verifiable Credentials (VCs):

    1. Health Authroity creates a Credential 

    2. Health Authroity signs Credential with their Identity to create Verifiable Credential

    2. Verifiable Credential information is encoded into a Zebra Smart Card

    3. Zebra Smart Card is presented to NFC capable smart device with verifier application installed to verify the information on the Smart Card

    4. Verification result (valid / invalid) is presented to operater via the smart device
